  • Elders Counsel

    The Elders are the leaders of the church and their responsibilities are to preach and teach, preside at the celebration of the sacraments, administer the church through pastoral guidance, and lead the congregations under their care in service ministry to the world. They Work within the community to advance God’s kingdom..

  • Outreach

    Outreach ministry is the practice of looking beyond yourself to the people around you and sharing the love of Christ as you serve and connect with your community. We believe in ministering to all people who are hurting and this includes all our brothers and sisters in Christ.

  • Youth Ministries

    The Youth Ministries offer a place for students to discover & develop a relationship with Christ. We focus our efforts on honoring God, unifying communities, and building people. We encourage them to discover their purpose. Know God. Find freedom. Make a difference.

  • Usher Board

    The Usher Board Ministry is responsible for maintaining a warm and safe environment during services at Light of Life. Our Ushers are tasked with the opportunity of being an extension of the Pastor. Ushers want to ensure that everyone who steps into Light of Life has a blessed and edifying experience.

  • Music Ministries

    Music is an integral part of our worship experience at Light of Life. Music Ministry has the responsibility of preparing and serving the music needs of our congregation. The primary purpose of the Music Ministry is to praise and worship God in the beauty of his holiness.
