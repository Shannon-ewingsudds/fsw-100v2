"Meet Our Pastor and First Lady"

Superintendent Roy W. Suuds and 1st Lady Corazon C. Sudds

Pastor and 1st Lady founded Life of Life Miracle Deliverance C.O.G.I.C. 37 years ago on Dec 14, 1984. They have been married for 42 years and have 2 children; 1 son and 1 daughter, 5 grandchildren, and 2 great-grandchildren.
